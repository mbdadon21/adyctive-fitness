FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to know how to dance to join?
Absolutely not. No dance experience required. Naarah makes her moves easy to pick up by the next class, and the atmosphere is completely judgment-free.
Can I join online if I'm not in Southern MD?
Yes! All classes are available live via Zoom. You'll get a link when you sign up and can join from anywhere in the world.
What should I bring to class?
Water (stay hydrated!), a towel, and your attitude. For Stretch n Flex, bring resistance bands, 1–3 lb weights, and a mat.
Are classes suitable for beginners?
100%. All fitness levels welcome. Naarah creates a safe, judgment-free space for everyone — whether it's your first class or your 500th.
Can I book a private party or fundraiser?
Yes! Adyctive Fitness can host your own Zumba® party or fitness fundraiser. Reach out through the form above to inquire.
